Massages

Massages are excellent for every period of the recovery - early, middle or final period.

Relaxing massage

Relaxing massage are good for people with tense muscles. Only with 30min procedure we can have a relaxed muscles and with better tone.

Тoning massage

Тoning massage are used when the muscles are weak and their tone should be improved. It is used mostly after bone fractures.

Healing massage

Healing massage are used when the client has a specific disease and the problem must be treated locally.

Specialized massage

Specialized massage used in our practise are chinese massage and acupressure. They are good when the client has a problem with some of the internal organs.

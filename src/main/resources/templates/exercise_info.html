Exercises

Exercise is an excellent tool for maintaining good health. Here we will describe some of the main types we use in our practice.

Exercises with weights

Exercises with machines and weights are good for people witch are in the final period of their recovery. Тhey are used to strengthen muscles and keep the body toned and healthy.

Exercises in water, swimming

Exercises in water or swimming are suitable for people who do not yet have strong enough muscles. For example when one client has a broken bone and after the immobilization period the muscles are atrophied and weak.

Treadmills and bicycles

Bicycle are an excellent tool for people with heart disease or varicose veins in the legs. Treadmill is great for overweight people.

Еxercises with own weight

Exercises with body weight are excellent for people in the middle of the recovery period. With these exercises you can begin to strengthen the body and muscles.
